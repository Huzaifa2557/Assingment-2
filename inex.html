Lahore

Lahore is the capital city of the Pakistani province of Punjab and is the country's second-largest city after Karachi.

Known as the cultural and culinary capital of Pakistan, Lahore is famous for its historical landmarks such as the Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort, and Shalimar Gardens.

The city has a rich cultural heritage, with vibrant festivals, traditional music, and a thriving arts scene.

Islamabad

Islamabad is the capital city of Pakistan.

It is known for its beautifully planned layout, with wide streets, greenery, and modern architecture.

Islamabad is home to important government buildings, diplomatic missions, and educational institutions.

The city offers various attractions such as Faisal Mosque, Pakistan Monument, and Shakarparian Park.

Karachi

Karachi is the largest and most populous city in Pakistan. It is the financial and economic hub of the country.

The city has a diverse culture, vibrant arts scene, and a rich historical heritage. Karachi is known for its beautiful beaches, bustling markets, and delicious street food.
